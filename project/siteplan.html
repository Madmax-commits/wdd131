Creative Pulse Site Plan

Site Name

Creative Pulse – This name reflects the vibrant and expressive nature of the arts, capturing the heartbeat of creativity across visual and performing disciplines. It’s a personal brand that represents my journey and passion for artistic expression.

Site Purpose

This website serves as a personal showcase and resource hub for visual and performing arts. It will feature:

  • A gallery of original artwork including drawings, paintings, and digital pieces.
  • Blog-style posts discussing artistic techniques, inspiration sources, and creative challenges.
  • A section highlighting local art events and exhibitions.
  • Tutorials and tips for beginners in various art forms (e.g., sketching, watercolor, digital illustration).
  • A contact page for collaboration or commissions.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find beginner-friendly tutorials for digital illustration?
  • Are there any upcoming art exhibitions in my area?

Color Schema

  • Deep Plum (#5D3A66) – Used for headings and accent elements.
  • Soft Cream (#F5F3E7) – Used as the background color for a warm, inviting feel.

Typography

  • Montserrat – Used for headings to give a modern, clean look.
  • Open Sans – Used for body text for readability and neutrality.
  • Dancing Script – Used for artistic flair in section titles or quotes.

Wireframes

Mobile View: Vertical scroll with stacked sections (gallery, blog, events, tutorials, contact).

Desktop View: Grid layout with horizontal navigation and featured content blocks.

Note: Wireframe sketches will be added as images or diagrams below.
